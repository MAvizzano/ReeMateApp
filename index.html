MacMate

Control your Mac

MacMate transforms your iPhone into a clean, responsive remote for your Mac — with quick access to media controls, pointer input, keyboard actions, and the companion experience that keeps everything connected.

Download MacMate on iPhone and MacMate Server on Mac to get started.

Download for Mac macOS 14.6+ · Direct download

Requires iOS 18.0 or later · macOS 14.6 or later

What you can do

Media

Volume & playback, instantly

Adjust volume, play, pause, and skip tracks on your Mac directly from your iPhone — without touching your keyboard.

Pointer

iPhone as a wireless trackpad

Move the cursor, click, scroll, and swipe using your iPhone screen as a precise wireless trackpad.

Keyboard

Type and trigger shortcuts

Send text, execute keyboard shortcuts, and trigger system actions on your Mac from anywhere in the room.
